About this area

Cavelossim

Located in Cavelossim, The Leela Goa is near a train station and on a private beach. The area's natural beauty can be seen at Mobor Beach and Varca Beach. Traveling with kids? Consider Maria Hall, or check out an event or a game at Fatorda Stadium. Spend some time exploring the area's activities, including golfing.
